The Beautiful Board Members of Lepo

Metropolian Leppävaaran Opiskelijat – LEPO ry

LEPO is a local student union operating in the Leppävaara unit of Metropolia University of Applied Sciences.

LEPO is a subdivision of the actual student organization METKA and represents METKA’s actions in Leppävaara.

Why?

At the time of writing, there are nearly fifteen hundred students in Leppävaara. Someone’s got to do it!

Our main tasks are to lobby, to act as a politically active component in Leppävaara and to relay information from METKA and other student unions elsewhere down to our fellow students in Leppävaara.

To the normal student this appears as taking care of selling tickets to various events, selling the student card stickers/stamps, organizing events,acting as a bridge between students and the staff of Leppävaara and whatever issue needs taking care of.

So what?

Everyone working for and with LEPO are volunteers and fellow students with an open mindset.
We don’t pay ourselves for doing this, but we feel that being able to do all these things we can do is a reward enough in itself.
Anybody is welcome to help us improve the study and social experiences for students in Leppävaara!
